A comparison of urban tree canopies and life expectancies

This unofficial comparison between Tacoma's urban tree canopies and residents' life expectancies is based on the city's Equity/Opportunity Index located on the City of Tacoma GeoHub Portal. On the left of the slider bar, U.S. Census block groups are color-coded by the density of the urban tree canopy. On the right of the slider bar, block groups are color-coded by life expectancy. While the Port of Tacoma appears to be an outlier, there is a clear correlation between urban tree canopy cover and life expectancy. However, the tree canopy is only one of several indicators within the Equity/Opportunity Index and shouldnâ€™t be considered a direct, causal factor to life expectancy.
